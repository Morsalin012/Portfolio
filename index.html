About Me

A little background on where I come from and where I'm going.

I am a Computer Science & Engineering undergraduate at Dhaka International University, graduating in August 2026. My work spans the full stack — from React-style frontend layouts and Node.js APIs all the way down to systems programming in C and lexical analysers built with Flex and GCC.
